FAQ

What services does Fanar Digital offer?
Fanar Digital offers a comprehensive range of services, including graphic design, website development, and video editing. We work with our clients to turn their ideas into tangible reality through innovative designs, high-quality websites, and professional videos that meet their marketing needs.
Can Fanar Digital develop responsive websites for all devices?
Yes, we ensure that we design and develop fully responsive websites for all devices, including computers, smartphones, and tablets. Our goal is to provide a seamless and consistent user experience across different screen sizes and devices.
How long does it take to complete a project?
The duration of a project depends on its size and complexity. Typically, simple design projects may take between 3 days to a week, while more advanced website development projects may require between one to two weeks. We always strive to adhere to the agreed-upon timelines with our clients.
What tools and software does Fanar Digital use?
We use a variety of advanced tools to ensure we deliver the best results for our clients. In graphic design, we utilize software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign to create creative and compelling designs. For web design and development, we rely on tools like Adobe XD, WordPress, React JS, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS to build exceptional and responsive websites. In video editing, we use advanced programs like Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, and Adobe Media Encoder to produce high-quality videos that reflect our clients' visions in the best possible way.
